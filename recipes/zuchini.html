Zuchini and Prawn Skewers

This recipe is perfect for these hot summer days

Ingredients

  • 12 praws
  • 2 zuchinis
  • 100g lamb's lettuce
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Wash the zucchinis, trim the ends, and slice them lengthwise using a mandoline.

  2. Remove the heads and shells from the prawns. Soak the skewers in water and wash the lamb’s lettuce.

  3. Assemble the skewers by alternating rolled zucchini slices with prawns. Season with salt and pepper, then brush with a little olive oil.

  4. Grill the skewers on a hot griddle for about 2 minutes on each side.

  5. Transfer them to plates and drizzle with the juice of half a lemon. Serve with lemon wedges and lamb’s lettuce.
